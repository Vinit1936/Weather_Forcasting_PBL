📖 How It Works

From Atmosphere to
Your Screen

A simplified look at how weather forecasting works.

01

Data Collection

Weather stations, satellites, radar systems, and ocean buoys collect atmospheric data every minute across the globe.

02

Data Processing

Raw data is cleaned, validated, and fed into numerical weather prediction models running on supercomputers.

03

Model Simulation

Atmospheric equations simulate future weather states using fluid dynamics, thermodynamics, and radiative transfer.

04

Forecast Delivery

The refined forecast is transformed into beautiful, easy-to-understand visuals and delivered straight to your device.
